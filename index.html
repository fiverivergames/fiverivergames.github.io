Hello!


I am an independent game designer focused on designing and developing a variety of fun adventures. I am currently based around Ontario, Canada. Having studied in Computer Science and Project Management, I have a vast variety of experiences I bring to the table.


Released Games

 
Dinos: Dodge annd Rescue
In Dinos: Dodge and Rescue, you play as Blu with the world ending and the sky falling. Baby Dino have been blasted into the sky and it is your job to save them, all the while dodging the falling rocks. How many babies can you save?
Dino Dart
In Dino Dart, you take on the role of Dart the Dino, attempting to survive extinction. A simple endless runner with multiple objectives including collecting eggs, saving baby Dino and outrunning extinction.
Road Rage: Road Race
In Road Rage, you play as a easily rage induced driver. A throwback to classic and minimal 2D arcade racing games. Drive down the highway while avoiding the police, other cars and road blocks.




Game Jams

 
Thirsty Crow
Jam: MISJam - Theme: Where there is a will, there is a way
In Thirsty Crow, you play as a thirsty crow who wanted to fly over the world who could not go far without stopping to drink water. After flying, he would feel weak, and start to lose hope and land. Can you help him get water and continue on his journey?

Sheeple
Jam: Toronto Game Jam 13 - Theme: Winning is for losers
In Sheeple, you play as a sheep who has always been told that winning is everything, from the day you are born. One day you begin to be put under a variety of different tests, to see who the best is. Little do you know that winning is actually for the losers.

Aleam Ludere - Play at Dice
Jam: Ludum Dare 41 - Theme: Combine 2 Incompatible Genres
In Aleam Ludere, you play as a dice wielding cowboy who happened to get caught cheating using loading dice. Now you must use your dice to survive and escape the casino. How lucky can you get?

A Cleansers Transmission - Global Game Jam 2018
Jam: Global Game Jam 2018 - Theme: Transmission
A 2D platformer where you are a sage attempting to transmit the holy spirit to cleanse the souls of the corrupt. Made in a group at Humble College for Global Game Jam 2018. Made In Unity
